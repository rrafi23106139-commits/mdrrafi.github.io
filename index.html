5. Engineering Projects

Structural Truss Analysis & Drafting

Structural Engineering

Objective: To perform comprehensive load analysis on structural truss systems, identifying tension and compression members to ensure stability.

Software Used: AutoCAD / Structural Analysis Tools

Methodology: Calculated internal forces (Tension/Compression) across multiple nodes using the method of joints/sections. Designed accurate engineering drafts to scale.

Results: Successfully mapped out critical stress points, ensuring the design meets structural integrity requirements.

Urban Flood Susceptibility Assessment

ART 203 Final Project | Gazipur District

Objective: To assess urban flood susceptibility using GIS and multi-criteria analysis.

Software Used: ArcGIS

Methodology: Integrated multiple spatial data layers including elevation, land use, drainage, and rainfall patterns.

Results: Created a comprehensive flood risk map essential for urban planning and disaster risk management.

Runoff Calculation & Floodplain Mapping

Engineering Hydrology (CEN 327)

Objective: To perform runoff calculation and map floodplain susceptibility.

Methodology: Utilized the SCS–CN method to analyze land use, soil characteristics, and rainfall to model surface runoff.

Results: Highlighted the vulnerability of low-lying regions, proving how data-driven modeling supports flood planning.
